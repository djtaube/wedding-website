Architecture

The Ewing couple which built and owned the manor was inspired by a trip around the world where they brought back many ideas and sketches for designing their residence. It is patterned after Channel-Norman style architecture mostly seen in England and France.

Hazle Buck Ewing

Born in 1880, Hazel lived for 88 years and had quite an interesting life. The first half included earning a Bachelor of Philosophy, marrying Davis Ewing, adopting a son and raising another, and then taking a trip around the world.

The second half began with the completion of the manor and a divorce from Davis after 20 years of marriage. For the next 30 years, Hazel devoted most of her time to philanthropic causes such as promoting international friendships, women's suffrage, and world peace.
